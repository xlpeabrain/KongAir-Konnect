## Why KongAir APIs?

- **Real-time operations** — pull schedules and seat availability refreshed from
  our aviation control systems.
- **Unified commerce** — manage bookings and ancillary services through a single
  integration.
- **Customer 360** — access loyalty status, preferences, and stored payment
  methods (with consent) to personalize every touchpoint.
